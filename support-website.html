‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the SOS feature?
When you open the app, you'll see a purple slider with a hand icon that says "Slide to speak to companion." Simply slide this from left to right to instantly connect with your AI companion for anxiety support.
Where can I find the SOS button?
The SOS feature is prominently displayed on the app's main screen (home screen) as an interactive slider. It's the primary feature you see when opening the app - no navigation required.
How do I add emergency contacts?
On the main screen, tap the "My Emergency Contact" button with the heart icon below the SOS slider. You can add a contact's name and phone number for quick access during emergencies.
Can I customize my companion?
Yes! Go to your profile settings to customize your companion's name, personality traits (friendly, empathetic, rational, etc.), and communication style to match your preferences.
Is my data private and secure?
Absolutely. Your conversations and personal information are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties and follow strict privacy guidelines.
Is this app a replacement for therapy?
No. My Companion provides emotional support and coping strategies but is not a substitute for professional mental health treatment. If you're experiencing severe mental health issues, please consult with a licensed therapist or counselor.
What should I do if the app isn't working?
Try closing and reopening the app, check your internet connection, and ensure you have the latest version installed. If problems persist, contact our support team.

üîß Technical Support

Common Issues & Solutions

  • App won't load: Check your internet connection and try restarting the app
  • SOS slider not working: Ensure you're sliding from left to right across the full width
  • Can't add emergency contact: Make sure to include the country code (e.g., +1 for US numbers)
  • Companion not responding: Check your internet connection and try again
